# Equipment Tier Progression

Equipment tiers define the long-term growth path for ReefIdle V3. Each tier improves carrying capacity, maintenance cost control, water stability, and income ceiling.

## Tier 1: Basic Equipment

Tier 1 builds the minimum Berlin system foundation.

- Filter sock: catches particulate waste and reduces early maintenance pressure.
- Protein skimmer: exports organic waste and improves oxygen exchange.
- Refugium: exports nutrients and creates basic biological stability.
- Return pump: enables sump circulation and basic water turnover.
- Live rock: improves biological filtration and livestock carrying capacity.
- Filter media: adds basic mechanical or biological support.
- Heater: stabilizes temperature and reduces livestock stress.

Tier 1 effects:

- Carrying capacity: low to moderate increase.
- Maintenance cost: moderate manual work remains.
- Water stability: basic stability, vulnerable to load spikes.
- Income ceiling: supports hardy starter livestock and low reef point output.

## Tier 2: Intermediate Equipment

Tier 2 adds environmental control and better nutrient management.

- Chiller: controls high temperature risk and protects sensitive livestock.
- UV sterilizer: reduces pathogen and water clarity pressure.
- Refugium light: improves refugium export efficiency.
- Wave pump: improves flow coverage and coral health.

Tier 2 effects:

- Carrying capacity: moderate to high increase.
- Maintenance cost: reduced losses and fewer instability events.
- Water stability: stronger protection from temperature, flow, and pathogen issues.
- Income ceiling: supports more valuable livestock and higher reef point output.

## Tier 3: Advanced Equipment

Tier 3 adds automation and advanced reef chemistry control.

- KH stabilizer: reduces alkalinity swing and supports SPS growth.
- Calcium reactor: maintains calcium and alkalinity for heavy coral load.
- Roller mat: automates mechanical filtration and reduces manual filter sock work.
- Bio pellet reactor: supports advanced nutrient control.
- ATO reservoir: automates evaporation top-off and salinity stability.

Tier 3 effects:

- Carrying capacity: high increase and better safe operating margin.
- Maintenance cost: lower manual cost but higher equipment operating cost.
- Water stability: strong stability for sensitive livestock.
- Income ceiling: unlocks high-value coral and advanced reef point scaling.
